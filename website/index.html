Game Features

Packed with exciting features to keep you engaged for hours

Fast-Paced Action

Engage in intense battles with increasing difficulty as you progress through levels. Every wave brings new challenges!

High Score System

Compete with yourself and track your progress with the encrypted high score system. Can you reach the top?

Intuitive Controls

Simple and responsive controls that are easy to learn but challenging to master. Perfect for all skill levels!

Epic Soundtrack

Immersive background music and sound effects create an engaging atmosphere. Toggle music and sounds on the fly!

Progressive Difficulty

Each level introduces faster aliens and more complex patterns. Test your reflexes as the challenge intensifies!

Customizable Experience

Adjust game settings, control volume, and pause whenever you need. Play at your own pace!

How to Play

Master the controls and dominate the battlefield

Game Controls

  • ← Move left
  • → Move right
  • Space Shoot
  • P Pause game
  • M Music on/off
  • S Sounds on/off
  • Q Quit game

Gameplay Tips

  • Destroy all aliens to advance to the next level and earn bonus points
  • Aliens move faster and shoot more frequently as you progress through levels
  • Don't let aliens reach the bottom - game over if they do!
  • You have limited lives - use them wisely and avoid enemy fire
  • Try to beat your high score and challenge yourself!

System Requirements

Minimum (Windows)

  • OS: Windows 10 (64-bit)
  • Processor: Dual-core CPU @ 2.0 GHz
  • Memory: 2 GB RAM
  • Graphics: OpenGL 3.0 compatible GPU
  • Storage: 250 MB available space

Recommended (Windows)

  • OS: Windows 10 or Windows 11 (64-bit)
  • Processor: Modern Dual-core CPU @ 2.5 GHz or better
  • Memory: 4 GB RAM
  • Graphics: Dedicated GPU with OpenGL 3.0 support
  • Storage: 250 MB available space

macOS

  • OS: macOS 10.13 (High Sierra) or newer
  • Processor: Intel or Apple Silicon (via Rosetta 2)
  • Memory: 4 GB RAM
  • Graphics: Metal or OpenGL compatible GPU
  • Storage: 250 MB available space

* The game is self-contained and does not require any external dependencies to run. Compatible with Windows and macOS.
