How to Play

Game Controls

  • ← Move left
  • → Move right
  • Space Shoot
  • P Pause game
  • Q Quit game

Gameplay Tips

  • Destroy all aliens to advance to the next level
  • Aliens move faster as you progress
  • Don't let aliens reach the bottom of the screen
  • You have limited lives - use them wisely
  • Try to beat your high score!
