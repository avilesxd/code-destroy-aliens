System Requirements

Minimum

  • OS: Windows 10 64-bit
  • Processor: Intel Core i3 or equivalent
  • Memory: 4 GB RAM
  • Graphics: Any modern graphics card with OpenGL support
  • Storage: 500 MB available space
  • Network: Broadband Internet connection

Recommended

  • OS: Windows 10/11 64-bit
  • Processor: Intel Core i5 or equivalent
  • Memory: 8 GB RAM
  • Graphics: Dedicated graphics card with OpenGL 2.0 or higher support
  • Storage: 1 GB available space
  • Network: Broadband Internet connection
  • Monitor: 1280x720 minimum resolution

* The game is only compatible with Windows 10 or later. For the best experience, use a dedicated graphics card and keep your drivers up to date.
